Milano Green Week 2024

Group project | 3 months

Project Overview

Design a flayer an event guide and an invite for an ecofriendly event in Milano (Milano Green Week 2024). Design a logo and find a slogan.

The creative process involved researching green design trends, focusing on natural colors, organic shapes, and clean typography. The logo represents two hands holding a tree symbolizing the role of humans in taking care of their planet. The slogan, "Together we grow green" reinforces the event’s mission to promote sustainability and to put the focus on the collaboration between humans. The flyer, event guide, and invite were designed with cohesive visuals and clear, engaging layouts to effectively communicate the event’s schedule and highlights.
