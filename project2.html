Euphoria website

Solo project | 3 months

Project Overview

Create a non responsive website based on a tv series. From paper sketches create wireframes and Mockups using Figma. Design a logo with Illustrator.

I was inspired by the TV series Euphoria, aiming to capture its distinctive aesthetic. The creative process involved deep research into the show's visual style, focusing on its bold use of colors, lighting, and dreamy atmosphere. The logo—a pill releasing glitter—symbolizes the series' central theme of drug use intertwined with its glittery, surreal visuals.
