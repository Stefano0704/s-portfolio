SpikeMaster App

Group project | 4 months

Project Overview

Mobile application for ubiquitous technology used in volleyball training sessions. User research, needfinding, creation of the product. From paper sketches create wireframes and Mockups using Figma. Logo design, focus on the UX and Usability.

As a volleyball player I was inspired by the potential of ubiquitous technology to enhance training sessions. The creative process involved conducting user research and needfinding to understand the specific requirements of coaches and players. The app's design focused on delivering a seamless user experience, with intuitive navigation and clear visual cues to improve usability. The logo, a dynamic silhouette of a volleyball in motion, captures the energy of the sport. The final product offers real-time feedback and analytics, empowering athletes to track progress and optimize performance.
