Symfonisk website

Group project | 3 months

Project Overview

Create a non responsive website based on a product from the Ikea catalogue 2020. From paper sketches create wireframes on Illustrator and then Mockups using Photoshop. Design a logo and edit all the images using Photoshop.

I was inspired by the clean and functional aesthetic of Ikea, aiming to reflect the brand's simplicity and modern design language. The creative process involved in-depth research into Ikea’s catalog, focusing on its signature use of white space, modular layouts, and vibrant yet balanced color schemes.Image editing in Photoshop emphasized enhancing product visuals, ensuring clarity and appeal while maintaining Ikea’s authentic look and feel.
